<h1></h1>
<p>I currently work as a Research Associate at Harvard Business School. I finished my M.A. in Economincs from Columbia University and completed my undergraduate degree in Economics from University of Mumbai.</p>
<p>My research interest lies at the intersection of labor, organisational and urban economics. I am interested in studying what drives individual decisions about remote versus in office work, their location decisions based on this as well as independently and the welfare implications of such choices. Tangentially, I am also interested in studying firm's decision to allow remote work and thus also production fragmentation. I am also interested in topics in Political Economy that deals with electoral politics and its impact on socio-economic outcomes. </p>
